System Initialization

Engineering Accuracy from Scratch.

The D.A.R.T. system represents a paradigm shift in autonomous ballistic deployment. Designed to bridge the gap between mechanical energy storage and digital precision, the platform utilizes advanced leadscrew actuation to deliver consistent results in high-stakes environments.

2) Problem Statement

Traditional projectile systems often suffer from energy loss and manual reloading bottlenecks. Our challenge was to eliminate these inefficiencies while maintaining a target window of 140mm at a distance of 26 meters.

Current student-led research often overlooks the structural integrity required for high-velocity recoil. D.A.R.T. solves this through custom-designed pitch and yaw mounts optimized for heavy load distribution.

Projectile: Mechanical Innovation

The airframe utilizes a "zonal" material approach. By 3D printing with TPU64D at the impact zone and PEBA90A for the core chassis, the projectile absorbs energy without structural failure.
